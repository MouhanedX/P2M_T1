# NQMS Fiber MongoDB Atlas Schema Design

## Database: `nqms_fiber_monitoring`

### Collections Overview

1. **rtus** - Remote Test Units registry
2. **routes** - Fiber optic routes configuration
3. **alarms** - Real-time alarms from RTUs
4. **measurements** - OTDR measurement traces
5. **kpis** - Calculated KPIs and aggregated metrics
6. **events** - System events and audit log

---

## Data Retention Policies

- **alarms**: Keep forever (or archive after 1 year)
- **measurements**: Keep 90 days, then archive to cold storage
- **kpis**: Keep 1 year
- **events**: Keep 6 months
- **rtus**: Keep forever
- **routes**: Keep forever

## Sharding Strategy (for scale)

- **alarms**: Shard by `{ rtu_id: 1, "lifecycle.created_at": 1 }`
- **measurements**: Shard by `{ route_id: 1, timestamp: 1 }`
- **kpis**: Shard by `{ kpi_type: 1, timestamp: 1 }`
